1. Introduction
 
    This software offers stock list, stock quotes, introday prices and historical price graph of China stock market, including Shanghai stock exchange and Shenzhen stock exchange. It also offers a tool for trading according to user-defined strategies.  
 
2. Functions
 
    (1) Investment strategy design.
    (2) Strategy-guide trading.
    (3) Strategy reports.
    (4) Stock quotes with sorted base information and marketing data.
    (5) User-defined stock-list and re-calculated items.
    (6) Domains and groups management and statistics.
    (7) Abundant mechanical indicators.
    (8) Dynamic-calculated historical price chart.
    (9) Predicate new data added into chart.
    (10) Data management.
